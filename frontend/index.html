What Is Phishing & How to Protect Yourself

Knowledge is the first line of defense. Phishing remains the most common entry point for cyberattacks globally.

warning Common Phishing Forms

  • check_circle

    Deceptive Phishing

    Emails impersonating legitimate brands to steal credentials or financial info.

  • check_circle

    Spear Phishing

    Highly personalized attacks targeting specific individuals within an organization.

  • check_circle

    Quishing (QR Phishing)

    Malicious links hidden in QR codes to bypass traditional email filters.

verified_user Best Practices

Check the Sender's Address

Always hover over the name to see the actual email address. Look for subtle misspellings.

Inspect Before You Click

Hover over links to see their true destination. If it doesn't match the text, don't click.

Enable Multi-Factor (MFA)

MFA provides a critical safety net even if your password is compromised via phishing.
